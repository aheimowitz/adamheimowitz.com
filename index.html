About

My name is Adam Heimowitz and I am a Software Developer and Tech Enthusiast.

I graduated from Binghamton Univeristy in the spring of 2014 and during my time there attempted to make as big of an impact as I could. I served as the President of my living community for a year, during which time my fellow e-board members and I were able to put on events that helped bring joy to all the students throughout the University. Our biggest event was Casino in the Woods where we transformed our Dining Hall into a real Casino for the night and donated all of the money we raised to the Red Cross. I also served as the Student Association Webmaster during my senior year which allowed me the chance to work with several student organizations to help bring some of their website ideas to life.

Today I work as a Software Developer for TD Ameritrade in Jersey City where I work along side the Fixed Income Group to help automate many of the processes that were previously done manually by the traders. Most recently, I built an order management system for new issue securities, which are securities that are just being released to market (ex. IPOs). Thus enabling the ability for business to process orders online through our website for these new issues, cutting down the processing time for allocating shares to clients from hours to seconds.
